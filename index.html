Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

Spaghetti
200g of dry spaghetti
Ground Beef
300g of ground beef
Tomato Sauce
1 cup of tomato sauce
Onion
1 medium onion, chopped
Garlic
2 cloves of garlic, minced
Olive Oil
2 tablespoons of olive oil
Salt and Pepper
To taste
Parmesan Cheese
Grated, for topping

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until soft.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain the pasta.
  5. Serve the spaghetti topped with the Bolognese sauce and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
